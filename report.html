Report a problem

Found a bug, a bad translation, or something that just doesn't feel right? Tell us what happened — reports go straight to the developer.

Reports are stored to be reviewed by the developer and are not shared with third parties.

Prefer email?

Write directly to gandribidav@gmail.com — same inbox this form delivers to.

Before you report

For translation issues, note the source text, the language pair, and which provider you had selected in Settings — it helps track the problem down faster.

Data & privacy

Submitting this form sends your report and optional email to the developer for the sole purpose of resolving the issue. See the Privacy Policy for details.
