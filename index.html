Features

Everything you need to actually remember new words

Atom Translate stores your vocabulary locally by default, so it works with no account and no connection — with cloud features available whenever you want them.

Offline-first storage

Folders, sections, words, and progress live in a local database on your device — no account required to start learning.

Optional cloud sync

Sign in when you want your folders, words, and progress synchronized across every device you own.

Community decks

Publish folders publicly, or download and clone decks other learners share, with your own progress tracked separately.

Configurable translation

Translate automatically, switch providers in Settings, or type every translation yourself in manual mode.

Speech-to-text & TTS

Dictate words and hear pronunciations using your device's built-in speech services.

Your data, exportable

Export your entire vocabulary to CSV at any time — your words are never locked into the app.

Study modes

Four ways to move words into long-term memory

Switch modes per session, or let a folder cycle through all four as your mastery level improves.

01

Flashcards

Flip through original and translation, and mark how well you knew each word.

02

Quiz

Pick the correct translation from multiple choice to test quick recall.

03

Writing

Type the translation from memory for the strongest retention.

04

Matching

Pair originals with translations against the clock.
